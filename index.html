In Honor and Inspiration

This initiative is built upon the memory and incredible talent of our beloved sister, Kristi. Her art will be woven into the fabric of "Other," serving as a beacon of creativity, expression, and the profound beauty of a unique mind.

Our Purpose

To build a world where neurodiversity is recognized, understood, and celebrated in every community.

Mission

To empower neurodivergent individuals and foster understanding within underserved and rural communities by providing accessible education on autism, ADHD, and other neurocomplexities. We aim to dismantle societal pressures to conform, celebrating authenticity and bridging the gap between neurodiversity and community acceptance, thereby preventing the despair of isolation and honoring every unique mind.

Vision

A world where neurodiversity is recognized, understood, and celebrated in every community, enabling all individuals, regardless of their neurology or location, to live authentically, thrive financially, and contribute their unique talents without fear of disparity or isolation.
